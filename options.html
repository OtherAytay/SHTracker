About

Overview

Build and track habits that are directly or indirectly related to the Sissy lifestyle! This tracker keeps a running list of habits you will and have developed across a variety of lifestyle areas relating to feminization, physique, and sexuality!

On your home page, you will see a card for each lifestyle area enabled. Inside the card, you will see a box containing a habit for each habit you have built in that area. When you reach a boundary for a benchmark or discretion type, you will see a visual separator.

Find a detailed spreadsheet of all available habits here.

Getting Started

To get started, check out the options panel and configure the habit tracker to best suit you. Changes you make to the options are saved automatically! If you are entirely sure what an option does, scroll down for an in-depth explanation.

The first time you use the tracker, you should do the following:
  1. Use the switches next to the various lifestyle areas to choose the categories of habit you would like to build.
  2. Determine what level of discretion you need when building these habits.
  3. Choose an allocation method. This will dictate whether you have control over point distribution.
  4. Choose whether you want to use benchmarks or not. See below for an explanation!
  5. Choose your allocation interval. This is how often you can assign points to habits.
  6. Choose how many points will be available to allocate each interval.

Option Explanations

Discretion
  • Private will limit the habits you build to only occur in a place you can maintain privacy.
  • Discrete allows for some habits to occur in public spaces, but they will generally be easier to hide or may not draw attention of others.
  • Public removes any restrictions on habits, meaning the habits may be outwardly and immediately visible to others in public. This is most suitable to individuals that have publicly declared their identity and are comfortable with Sissy presentation in public spaces.
Allocation Method

Choice allows you the freedom to choose which lifestlye areas each of your available habit points will go. Random will show a button on the habit page that will distribute your available points randomly across your enabled areas.

Benchmarks

Benchmarks are specific levels of each lifestyle area that represent a natural stopping point in your habit progress. When a lifestyle area reaches a benchmark, you will not be able to allocate further points into that lifestyle area until all other enabled areas have also reached that same benchmark. This is best used if you want to more evenly distribute points across your different lifestyle areas.

Allocation Interval

This is how often you will be able to allocate points. When you finish allocating points, a waiting period will trigger until you can allocate points again. Please note that the allocation waiting period will begin after the last point available has been allocated. Open allows you allocate points whenever you want!
